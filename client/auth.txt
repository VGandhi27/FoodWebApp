How Auth0 works
    GO TO Auth0.COM - SIGNUP/CREATE ACCOUNT - GO TO APPLICATIONS - CREATE AND CHOOSE A PROJECT - AND GO TO SETTINGS 
    NOW IN SETTINGS YOU WILL FIND DOMAIN ID REPLACE THAT WITH YOUR ONE IN APP.JS FILE.
    NOW GO TO AUTHENTICATION - GO TO SOCIAL AND THERE YOU FIND GOOGLE AUTH AND YOU CAN CREATE MANY MORE SOCIAL AUTHENTICATION BY CLICKING ON CREATE CONNECTION
